✨ V-ing 现在分词/动名词

  • 进行时 be + V-ing → She is reading.
  • 主动定语 a rising sun (正在升起的)
  • 动名词主语 Reading helps.
  • 介词后 Think before leaving.
  • 感官主动 I saw him running.
  • 伴随状语 Hearing the news, he cried.

🧱 V-ed 过去分词

  • 被动语态 be + V-ed → English is spoken.
  • 完成时 have/has + V-ed → She has finished.
  • 被动定语 a broken glass (被打碎的)
  • 被动完成 get sth. done → get it repaired.
  • 固定搭配 be interested in
  • 原因状语(被动) Surprised by the result, he shouted.
